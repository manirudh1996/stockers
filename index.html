How to read this chart
Shaded band — 5th to 95th percentile: full range of simulated outcomes
Green line — Bull Case (75th percentile)
Navy line — Base Case (50th percentile): the central expected outcome
Red line — Bear Case (25th percentile)
Faint lines — 20 individual sample paths. Each path shows one possible price journey. Bands hide the messiness; paths reveal it.
